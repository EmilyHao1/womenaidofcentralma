Events

Since its inception in 2006, we’ve hosted several Womenaid events . Each event is different but the theme is the same. Come to the event, donate what you would spend on a night out, and enjoy the food, drink, and festivities of Womenaid. At the event, you’ll learn how Womenaid has recently used its funds to aid others in the community. We welcome you to attend our next event, and feel free to bring others—the more the merrier!
